Praia Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site name, "Praia Chamber of Commerce," was selected to reflect the focus on promoting business and community engagement in Praia. It represents the city and the Chamber's mission to foster economic development.

Site Purpose

The website will serve two primary purposes:

  • Provide a platform for businesses in Praia to network, grow, and collaborate.
  • Offer resources and information about local economic growth, community events, and business policies.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events will help businesses in Praia network and promote growth?
  • Where can I find the list of the chamber's board members and their contact information?
  • What are the current economic trends and population growth statistics for Praia?

Color Schema

The selected colors for the website are:

  • Dark Blue: Used for headers and the navigation bar, reflecting trust and professionalism.
  • Light Gray: Used for background colors to provide contrast without overwhelming the viewer.

Typography

We will use the following fonts:

  • Montserrat: For headings, to make them stand out and modern.
  • Open Sans: For body text, ensuring readability and a clean look.
